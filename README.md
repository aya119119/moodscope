### MoodScope

MoodScope is a web application that connects to your Spotify account, fetches your liked songs, and uses machine learning to automatically group them into mood-based playlists.
The app analyzes Spotify's audio features for each track (such as energy, valence, danceability, acousticness, and others) and applies clustering to organize songs by similar "vibe." Playlists are created directly in your Spotify library.This project focuses on a data-driven approach to music organization, with transparency into the clustering process.
